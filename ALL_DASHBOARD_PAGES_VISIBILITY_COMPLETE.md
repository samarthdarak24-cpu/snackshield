## 🎨 DESIGN SYSTEM APPLIED

### Colors
- **Backgrounds**: `bg-white` (was `bg-surface-2/50`)
- **Text**: 
  - Headings: `text-slate-900 font-black` (was `text-white font-semibold`)
  - Body: `text-slate-600 font-semibold` (was `text-slate-400`)
  - Labels: `text-slate-900 font-bold` (was `text-slate-300 font-medium`)
- **Borders**: `border-2 border-slate-200` (was `border border-white/5`)
- **Accents**: 
  - Purple: `from-purple-600 to-purple-500`
  - Emerald: `from-emerald-600 to-emerald-500`
  - Red: `from-red-600 to-red-500`

### Typography
- **Headings**: `font-black` (900 weight)
- **Subheadings**: `font-bold` (700 weight)
- **Body**: `font-semibold` (600 weight)
- **Labels**: `font-bold` (700 weight)

### Icons
- **Size**: 22-24px (was 18-20px)
- **Stroke**: `strokeWidth={2.5}` or `strokeWidth={3}` (was default)

### Borders
- **Thickness**: `border-2` or `border-4` (was `border` = 1px)
- **Color**: `border-slate-200` or `border-slate-300` (was `border-white/5`)

### Shadows
- **Cards**: `shadow-xl` (was none or minimal)
- **Buttons**: `shadow-lg` (was `shadow-lg shadow-primary-500/25`)
